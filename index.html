What Nebula Does

Nebula Launcher does NOT replace Minecraft. It acts as a control layer that:

  • Detects your installed Minecraft launcher
  • Launches vanilla Minecraft automatically
  • Applies mod profiles (planned feature)
  • Keeps everything lightweight and fast

How It Works

  1. Install Nebula Launcher (.msi)
  2. Open the app
  3. Select Minecraft version/profile
  4. Click Play → it opens vanilla launcher

Important

Nebula does not modify Minecraft files directly. It simply launches and configures the official launcher.
