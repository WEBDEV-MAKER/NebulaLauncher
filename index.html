What Nebula Launcher Does

Nebula Launcher is a lightweight control layer for Minecraft. It does NOT replace the vanilla launcher.
  • Detects installed Minecraft launcher
  • Launches vanilla Minecraft automatically
  • Supports future mod profiles
  • Designed to stay extremely lightweight

How It Works

1. Install Nebula Launcher
2. Open the app
3. Choose version/profile
4. Click Play → opens official Minecraft launcher
