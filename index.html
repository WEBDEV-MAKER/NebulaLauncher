What Nebula Launcher Does

Nebula Launcher does NOT replace Minecraft. It acts as a lightweight control layer that:
  • Detects installed Minecraft launcher
  • Launches vanilla Minecraft automatically
  • Prepares mod profiles (future feature)
  • Keeps performance extremely lightweight

How It Works

1. Install Nebula Launcher
2. Open the app
3. Select your Minecraft setup
4. Click Play → launches official Minecraft launcher
Important:
Nebula does not modify Minecraft files. It simply launches and configures the official launcher environment.
